CalConnect — designated by ISO as the Registration Authority
(ISO 24229/RA) — has stood up the official register of written language
conversion systems at **www.iso24229.org**. The register is operated by
Ribose.

## What is now available

- **The register itself**, with the first entries seeded from the
  historical CalConnect/CC transcription-systems work. Browse it at
  [/register](/register).
- **The Apply section**, with structured GitHub issue templates for
  existing authorities registering new systems and for new authorities
  applying to join. See [/apply](/apply).
- **The specification section**, including the
  [Terms of Reference (Annex A)](/specification/annex-a) and our
  [ISO 19135 compliance mapping](/specification/iso-19135).
- **Reference data**, with weekly auto-sync of
  [ISO 639](https://github.com/iso24229/iso639-data) and
  [ISO 15924](https://github.com/iso24229/iso15924-data) from their
  authoritative sources (Library of Congress, SIL, Unicode Consortium).

## What's next

The Advisory Group (ISO 24229/AG) will hold its inaugural meeting in the
coming weeks. The agenda will include ratification of the seeded
register entries and the operational rules published on this site.
Minutes will be published at [/managers/meetings](/managers/meetings)
once ratified.

## Get in touch

Existing authorities that wish to register additional systems, or
organisations considering becoming a conversion system authority, can
begin the process at [/apply](/apply).
