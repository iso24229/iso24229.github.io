CalConnect — designated by ISO as the Registration Authority
(ISO 24229/RA) — has established the official register of written
language conversion systems at **www.iso24229.org**. The register is
operated by Ribose.

## What is now available

- **The register**, with initial entries derived from the historical
  CalConnect transcription-systems corpus. Browse it at
  [/register](/register).
- **The submission process**, with interactive forms for existing
  authorities registering new systems and for institutions applying to
  become new authorities. See [/about/apply](/about/apply).
- **The specification**, including the
  [Terms of Reference](/specs/ag-tor) and the
  [ISO 19135 compliance mapping](/specs/iso-19135).
- **Reference data**, with weekly synchronisation of
  [ISO 639](https://github.com/iso24229/iso639-data) and
  [ISO 15924](https://github.com/iso24229/iso15924-data) from their
  authoritative sources (Library of Congress, SIL International, Unicode
  Consortium).

## What's next

The Advisory Group (ISO 24229/AG) will convene its inaugural meeting
in the coming weeks. The agenda will include ratification of the
seeded register entries and review of the operational procedures
published on this site.

## Contact

Existing authorities that wish to register additional systems, or
organisations seeking to become conversion system authorities, may
begin the process at [/about/apply](/about/apply).
